Zaiqa Restaurant has been serving the people of Mirpur Mathelo with honest, homestyle cooking — the kind that reminds you of your mother's kitchen and keeps you coming back.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily using traditional recipes passed down through generations, with spices sourced locally and love added in every pot.
